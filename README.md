Sevagan is a SaaS platform designed to digitize and automate operations for local service businesses through a WhatsApp-first operating model.

The platform enables service providers such as electricians, plumbers, appliance repair technicians, AC service providers, cleaning companies, pest control operators, and maintenance businesses to manage customer acquisition, job scheduling, technician dispatch, invoicing, payments, customer communication, and repeat business from a single platform.

Unlike traditional CRM and Field Service Management (FSM) systems, Sevagan is designed specifically for India's highly fragmented local service economy, where WhatsApp serves as the primary communication channel and operational system.

Sevagan combines:

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
Field Service Management (FSM)
WhatsApp Business Automation
AI-Powered Operations Assistance
Mobile Workforce Management
Revenue Recovery Automation

The long-term vision is to become the operating system powering millions of local service businesses across India and emerging markets.

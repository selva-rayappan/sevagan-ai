About Sevagan

Redefining convenience — Sevagan was founded to bridge the trust gap in home services. We combine technology, empathy, and unmatched professionalism to deliver services that feel effortless. Each professional undergoes rigorous verification and skill assessment, ensuring you get nothing but the best.

What sets Sevagan apart is our obsession with customer delight. We don't just assign any professional — our AI-driven matching system pairs you with the right expert based on the specific job, past ratings, and proximity. This means faster arrival times and better first-time resolution rates.

Looking ahead, Sevagan aims to expand to 25+ cities by 2027 and introduce subscription-based maintenance plans for complete home care. Our vision is to become the invisible infrastructure that keeps Indian homes running smoothly — one doorstep at a time.
